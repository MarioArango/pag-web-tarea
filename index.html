BOWLING IN THE DEEP’S STORY


  1. 2013 - Rob invests and starts constructing Bowling on the Beach
  2. 2014 - Opening of Bowling on the Beach
  3. 2020 -Construction starts to renovate and expand
  4. 2021 - Bowling in the Deep grand opening

Today - Bowling in the Deep is working to expand to California and other cities


In 2020, Rob Martini, owner and founder of the newly built Bowling in the Deep, wanted to expand his passion for bowling and love of the ocean into an all inclusive bowling alley. Rob already had a bowling alley directly on Miami Beach, named Bowling on the Beach which was built in 2014, but after 7 years Rob wanted to not only renovate, but design a completely new bowling alley where the building is encapsulated around the actual ocean.


Being already located centrally on Miami Beach, Rob was able to capture the atmosphere of actually being in the ocean by creating an expansive aquarium surrounding 40 new synthetic bowling lanes, making this also the biggest bowling alley in all of Florida as well! Our new scoring system and pinsetters makes it perfect for bowlers of any skill level to have fun.


While bowling you can see a variety of oceanic wildlife including stingrays, sharks, and even dolphins. Across the bowling alley is our new bar and grill, which offers plenty of seafood options on the menu and comes fresh from the ocean daily. On the bar are plenty of TV’s where you can watch the latest sports games and downstairs features an extensive arcade for all age groups including air hockey and pool tables. This has quickly become Miami’s newest and biggest tourist attraction yet and we are hoping to expand to California as well as other cities soon! So if you’re planning on visiting Miami or Miami Beach, please feel free to stop by to bowl or to just see some oceanic wildlife. Doors are free to enter!

Who We Are


We have over 20 employees at our new and renovated Underwater Bowling! Our team consists of open minded, creative and hardworking people with love for bowling and customer service.

Rob Martini - Owner and Founder

Rob founded Bowling on the Beach on 2013, but not long after he decided his main goal was to provide a unique experience which would attract and retain loyal customers. He is always on site checking on his team and clients, as he believes being present is key in leadership and success.

Jared Bass - Accounting Manager

Jared started working with the owner Rob on 2013, and has always handled the numbers of the business, suggesting changes and initiatives to maximize profit.

Caroline Trey - Design Head

Caroline was hired in 2020 for our renovation, and she hit the spot every time with her creative ideas and distinctive approach, she’s been key to our new image, and we are currently on exciting new things!

Cade Morgan - Bowling Specialist

Cade was hired in 2021, because we wanted to maximize customer’s experience at the bowling. He oversees bowling operations while it’s open, ensuring, safety, rule abiding, questions, etc. Our customers have created great relationships with him as he enjoys teaching interesting new bowling techniques, tips, and tricks.

Delaney Rose - Marketing Manager

Delaney joined the team in 2020, helping us create the Marketing plan for our new grand opening. She oversees our social media, creates contests, events, and ensure customers engagement. She’s in charge of 2 other marketing specialists, who joined our team a few months after her.

Carson Bain - Bar Manager

Carson is the heart of the bar at Underwater Bowling, he created our new drinks menu and updates our special drink weekly, plans happy hours and is in charge of all bar operations, overseeing our 4 bartenders.

Ryan Tate - Chef

Ryan is an exceptional chef with experience in 6 different countries which has led to a big creative mind when it comes to food at our Underwater Bowling. He is in charge of 8 sous chefs that serve to over 150 people on a daily basis.
